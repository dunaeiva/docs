The `editor` role provides permissions to manage any Yandex Cloud [resources]({{ link-docs }}/resource-manager/concepts/resources-hierarchy) except assigning roles to other users, transferring [organization]({{ link-docs }}/organization/concepts/organization) ownership, deleting an organization, and deleting [encryption keys]({{ link-docs }}/kms/concepts/index) in Key Management Service.

For instance, users with this role can create, modify, and delete resources.

This role includes the `viewer` permissions.
