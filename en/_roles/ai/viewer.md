The `ai.viewer` role enables viewing info on quotas for [Yandex Translate]({{ link-docs-ai }}translate/concepts/limits#translate-quotas), [Yandex Vision OCR]({{ link-docs-ai }}vision/concepts/limits#vision-quotas), [Yandex SpeechKit]({{ link-docs-ai }}speechkit/concepts/limits#speechkit-quotas), and [Yandex AI Studio]({{ link-docs-ai }}ai-studio/concepts/limits#yandexgpt-quotas), on [text generation models]({{ link-docs-ai }}ai-studio/concepts/generation/models), [datasets]({{ link-docs-ai }}ai-studio/concepts/resources/dataset), uploaded [files]({{ link-docs-ai }}ai-studio/concepts/search/vectorstore#file-uploading), and Vector Store [search indexes]({{ link-docs-ai }}ai-studio/concepts/search/vectorstore) in Yandex AI Studio, read such files and conduct searches using such indexes, as well view info on the relevant [cloud]({{ link-docs }}/resource-manager/concepts/resources-hierarchy#cloud) and [folder]({{ link-docs }}/resource-manager/concepts/resources-hierarchy#folder).

This role includes the `ai.auditor`, `ai.assistants.viewer`, `ai.datasets.viewer`, and `ai.models.viewer` permissions.
