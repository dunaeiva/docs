The `serverless.mcpGateways.editor` role enables creating, modifying and deleting [MCP servers]({{ link-docs-ai }}ai-studio/concepts/mcp-hub/index#servers), as well as viewing info on them and [access permissions]({{ link-docs }}/iam/concepts/access-control/roles) granted for them.

This role includes the `serverless.mcpGateways.viewer` permissions.
